Mobile Collector

  • In order to prevent a flood of location and signal strength updates, minimum thresholds should be set to limit the frequency of callbacks. However, if the threshold is too large then the update density will be too low. Our design utilizes a minimum distance of 10 meters and broke the signal strength into 4 sections, with an update occuring only between sections. Additionally, we limited the update frequency to 2 minutes, though in practice this ended up being too high. A much lower recommended time such as 10 to 30 seconds is recommended.
